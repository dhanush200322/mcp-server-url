ZEA CRM is an all-in-one platform combining a traditional CRM with AI-powered workflow automation. It replaces the need for multiple disconnected tools — MailChimp, Constant Contact, Zapier, separate scheduling apps, and call centres — by consolidating everything into a single intelligent platform.



Core Value Proposition



ZEA CRM's #1 job is to ensure no lead is ever lost or forgotten. It achieves this through:



Immediate AI response to every new inquiry — within 5 minutes, 24/7

Multi-channel automated follow-up: email, SMS, WhatsApp, voice call, social DM

Intelligent nurturing through the entire sales lifecycle — from first touch to post-job review

Automated appointment scheduling synced to live calendar availability

4.1  'No Response' Path

No response after New Lead Funnel → moves to Follow-Up Funnel (30-day sequence)

No response after Follow-Up Funnel → moves to Annual Funnel (monthly touch, indefinitely)

Prospect says 'not interested' → moves immediately to Annual Funnel

Annual Funnel positive response → automatically re-enters Appointment Funnel



4.2  AI On/Off Logic

AI is ON by default at every automated stage

AI is OFF during human-interaction stages: site visit, live demo, negotiation

Client or sales team can manually override AI at any stage at any time

When AI hits a question it cannot answer, it alerts the human team and logs the gap for retraining

$499–$599/month is too expensive

Compare it to a full-time salesperson who costs $2,000–$4,000/month and only works 8 hours a day. ZEA works 24/7, never misses a follow-up, and handles unlimited leads simultaneously. One recovered deal pays for 12 months.

$3,000/year feels like a lot

Break it down: $19.96/day. If ZEA books even one extra appointment per month, the ROI is immediate. We also have the $499 limited plan to start smaller with a lower commitment.

We need to see it working before committing

Absolutely — we encourage that. We offer a 15-day CRM trial, and we can build 1–2 live proof-of-concept workflows for 10–15 days. We also have existing clients happy to provide references.

We're too deep into our current platform to switch

You don't need to switch. ZEA can run as a parallel automation layer — takes data from your existing system, processes it through our AI workflows, and pushes results back. Your team keeps using what they know.

AI might give wrong answers to our clients

ZEA uses ChatGPT-4 — the world's most tested AI. It only answers from your approved knowledge base. If it cannot answer a question, it routes to a human immediately and never guesses. All AI responses are logged for your review.

I can build my own AI agent for less

A standalone chatbot is possible, but ZEA is a complete 13-stage workflow automation system: lead capture, nurturing, scheduling, proposals, objection handling, job tracking, reviews, and long-term retention. Building that from scratch would take months and cost far more.

Why aren't you using AI for your own outreach?

We do. AI handles our lead research, qualification, and campaign management. We use the same system for our own outreach — UK and international rollout is happening now.

How do you prevent my competitors getting the same advantage?

Each client gets a separate sub-account with isolated data and brand voice. We also operate by territory — we do not onboard direct competitors in the same geographic area.

We want leads generated, not just managed

ZEA manages and nurtures your existing leads AND we offer a separate lead generation service: AI-powered email and LinkedIn outreach that identifies prospects in your target market and delivers qualified leads to your pipeline.

Clients prefer the personal touch — they don't want AI

ZEA never replaces human relationships. It handles repetitive, time-consuming work (initial responses, reminders, follow-ups) so your team has MORE time for high-value personal interactions. Clients only know it's AI if you tell them.

Compliance risk in UAE — what if AI causes a problem?

We use ChatGPT-4 only — not Grok or any model with compliance concerns. The AI is trained strictly on your approved content and never speculates. Any unanswered question triggers an immediate human handoff. All responses are logged.

9. Frequently Asked Questions



Designed for AI agent training, website chatbot, sales team reference, and client onboarding.



▸  Q: What is ZEA CRM?

ZEA CRM is an AI-powered workflow automation and CRM platform built specifically for smart home automation and AV integration businesses. It automates the entire sales process — from first lead inquiry to post-job review — using AI to handle follow-ups, appointment scheduling, and customer service across email, SMS, WhatsApp, and voice calls.



▸  Q: Do I need to replace my existing CRM?

No. ZEA integrates with your existing CRM via API — including Zoho, Bitrix24, Odoo, and others. Your data stays in place, your team keeps using what they know, and ZEA adds intelligent automation on top.



▸  Q: How quickly does the AI respond to a new lead?

Within 5 minutes of a lead being submitted — any time of day or night, including weekends and public holidays. The AI sends an initial personalised message and begins the follow-up sequence automatically.



▸  Q: What AI does ZEA use?

ZEA uses ChatGPT-4 by OpenAI. We do not use Grok or other models. ChatGPT-4 is approved for deployment in all markets including the UAE and Middle East.



▸  Q: Can the AI answer and make phone calls?

Yes — both inbound and outbound. The inbound AI answers missed calls via Twilio, introduces itself as your AI assistant, answers from your knowledge base, and books appointments. The outbound AI proactively calls leads to schedule appointments. It can serve as your main number for after-hours and weekend coverage.



▸  Q: What if the AI doesn't know the answer?

The AI will never guess or give irrelevant answers. It tells the client that a team member will follow up, then immediately notifies your team with the unanswered question. That question is then added to the knowledge base for future training.



▸  Q: How does the review system work?

After job completion, the AI sends a review request to your client. If they give 5 stars, it is automatically posted to your Google Business page. If they give less than 5 stars, the review is held in your internal dashboard so you can address the issue before it goes public.



▸  Q: Can ZEA send WhatsApp messages?

Yes. ZEA integrates with WhatsApp Business API. It sends automated follow-ups, appointment reminders, proposals, and payment links via WhatsApp — and captures all inbound WhatsApp replies into the client's CRM record.



▸  Q: Is there a free trial?

Yes — the CRM platform has a 15-day free trial. Workflow automations are not included in the trial as they require our development team to build. We can also set up 1–2 live proof-of-concept workflows for a 10–15 day test run before full commitment.



▸  Q: How long does onboarding take?

Typically 1–2 weeks for standard setup. This includes account configuration, CRM setup, building 5 core automated workflows, AI agent training, and team onboarding. Complex setups with third-party integrations may take 2–3 weeks.



▸  Q: Who builds and manages the workflows?

ZEA builds all workflows for you. You tell us your process, your Q&As, and your preferences — we configure everything. You can request changes at any time and we implement them. Most clients prefer this so they can focus on running their business, not managing software.



▸  Q: What happens to a lead that says 'not interested'?

They move to the Annual Funnel — a long-term nurturing sequence that sends one personalised email and one SMS per month, indefinitely. The moment they respond positively, they are automatically re-routed back to the Appointment Funnel. Many clients come back 6–12 months later through this funnel.



▸  Q: Can I use this for existing clients, not just new leads?

Absolutely. ZEA has dedicated post-sale funnels: Annual Maintenance Contract renewal reminders, post-care check-ins at 30/60/90 days, review and referral funnels, and the Weather Funnel for storm alerts. These generate repeat business and referrals from your existing customer base.



▸  Q: Is my client data secure?

Yes. Each client gets a completely separate sub-account with fully isolated data. We do not share data between clients. Client workflows, contact data, and business intelligence are never shared with competitors or third parties.



▸  Q: What is the pricing?

Full Funnels Plan: $599/month. Limited Funnels Plan: $499/month. CRM Only: from $97/month. One-time setup fee: $999 (standard) or $1,499 (with third-party CRM integration). No annual maintenance fees. Website chatbot included free with any full plan.



▸  Q: What support is available after onboarding?

Our team is available 18–20 hours per day across our India and Florida offices. All queries are addressed within 24 hours via email, WhatsApp, or LinkedIn. Workflow changes can be requested at any time.
